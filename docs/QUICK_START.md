# HotelOS Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to HotelOS!

This guide will help you get started with HotelOS in 30 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Login (2 min)

1. Go to your HotelOS URL
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

> üí° **Tip**: Staff can use their 4-digit PIN for quick login

---

## Step 2: Start Your Shift (3 min)

Before taking payments, you must start a shift:

1. Go to **Shifts & Cash** from sidebar
2. Count the cash in your drawer
3. Enter **Opening Cash Amount**
4. Click **Start Shift**

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Important**: You cannot logout until your shift is closed

---

## Step 3: Create a Booking (5 min)

1. Click **+ New Booking** (or use mobile FAB button)
2. Enter guest mobile number
3. Select or create guest profile
4. Choose dates and select available room
5. Enter advance payment (optional)
6. Click **Confirm Booking**

---

## Step 4: Check-In Guest (2 min)

1. Go to **Front Desk** ‚Üí **Today Arrivals**
2. Find your booking
3. Click **Check In**
4. Room status changes to "Occupied"

---

## Step 5: Check-Out & Invoice (5 min)

1. Go to **Front Desk** ‚Üí **Today Departures**
2. Click on the booking
3. Click **Checkout**
4. Review bill summary
5. Collect any pending amount
6. Click **Complete Checkout**
7. Print or download invoice

---

## Step 6: Close Your Shift (3 min)

At end of your duty:

1. Go to **Shifts & Cash**
2. Count cash in drawer
3. Click **End Shift & Handover**
4. Enter **Actual Closing Cash**
5. Select who to hand over to
6. Note any discrepancies
7. Click **Confirm Close**
